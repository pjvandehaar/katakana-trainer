Extra notes:

A miniature ッ (tsu) after a kana ends it with a glottal stop. This can be written in English as doubling the next consonant. Eg: アッカ (akka)

Any other miniature replaces/modifies the previous vowel. Eg: キャ (kya)

A ー makes the previous vowel longer. Eg: アー (aaaaa)
